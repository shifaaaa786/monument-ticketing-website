Albert Hall Museum

Location

Jaipur, Rajasthan
Timings

The museum is open for a visit on all the days of the week from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. For night visits, the timings are 7:00 PM to 10:00 PM throughout the week.
Entry Fee

20 per head for Indian Nationals and Rs.150 per head for foreign nationals.
Famous for

History, Photography, Architecture, Relics and Artefacts.
